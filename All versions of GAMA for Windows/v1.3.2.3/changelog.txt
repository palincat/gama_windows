Force Vulkan rendering
Soft-crash SystemUI, Settings, Samsung Launcher, AOD Services and GBoard.
Here's what GAMA-OpenGL will do:

Force OpenGL rendering
Soft-crash SystemUI, Settings, Samsung Launcher, AOD Services and GBoard.
